WHO WE ARE

Pioneering the Maghreb Since 2012

Maghreb Investment Holding AG was founded in 2012 by Roland Zanotelli, today's Chairman. One of the earliest pioneers in believing in the immense potential of the Maghreb region, not only because of its enormous capacity for development but also because of its incredible opportunities due to its proximity to Europe.

Today we are major shareholders of P&F Tunis, a real estate company that focuses on commercial and residential developments; and NAREC (North African Renewable Energy Company) leader in the renewable energy sector. Through our network, we are also actively and continuously supporting large foreign companies establish and grow in the region.

WHAT WE CAN DO FOR YOU

Our Services

Real Estate Projects

Through our controlled P&F Tunis, we focus on commercial and residential developments. In commercial centres, P&F Tunis collaborates with our partners INDIGO and UTIC.

Renewable Energies

Through NAREC — the North African Renewable Energy Company — we develop photovoltaic energy projects and are one of the leading distributors of solar equipment in Tunisia.

Commercial Intermediation

We actively support large foreign companies to establish and grow in the region. We offer cultural and commercial intermediation, guidance, and local representation across the Maghreb.
